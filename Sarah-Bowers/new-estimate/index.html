Notes & Conditions
  • Tub unit excluded: Client supplies or separately procures the Ella's Bubbles Royal Walk-In Tub (stainless steel door). S.B.R. LLC can assist with sourcing upon request.
  • Site verification required: Final pricing confirmed after on-site inspection of existing plumbing, electrical panel, and bathroom dimensions.
  • Water heater capacity: The Ella Royal holds up to 100 gallons. Existing water heater adequacy must be confirmed — upgrade quoted separately if needed.
  • Tub footprint: 31.5" × 52" × 35" with inward-swing door requiring 17.5" of swing clearance. Entry clearance to be verified prior to installation.
  • Window permit: Window modification may require a separate structural permit — included in Section 07 estimate; confirmed at time of permit application.
  • Scope exclusions: Structural modifications, subfloor replacement beyond leveling, mold remediation, and drywall repair beyond standard patching are not included.
  • Code & permits: All work performed to Washington State building code. All permits pulled by S.B.R. LLC.
  • Labor warranty: 1-year warranty on all work by S.B.R. LLC. Ella's Bubbles lifetime limited warranty applies to the tub unit separately.
